Resource linking with Hodder Share

One of the easiest ways to create a Hodder Share is to Login and use the free Generator to create a Hodder Share link from the URL of a resource that you have found

You can also use the Hodder Share Maker by dragging the text to your browser's toolbar. Then, when ever you find a resource anywhere on the web, click the toolbar link to post the URL of the page you're on to Hodder Share as a new, shareable link, ready to be added to your lessons and activities.

Hodder Share as a Community

Every time we receive a request for a new Hodder Share we'll check first to see if anyone else in the Hodder Share community has already created one for the resource you're linking too.

We want to create useful data about which resources Teachers find useful and share it with our publisher partners so that together the community will generate more of the resources teachers really want.

Share Hodder resources with your Class

Login to Hodder Share with your Google Classroom or 0365 for Education Team credentials.

Once logged in you can use Hodder Share alongside the tools you use everyday to create engaging lessons and activities, safe in the knowledge that the contents of the page provided by the Hodder Share is checked to ensure the end point is still valid before the page is served to the pupil.
