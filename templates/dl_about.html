Common Questions

What is Hodder Share?

A Hodder Share is a shortened, permanent link to online educational resource. Teachers can share them with their pupils and fellow teachers. Post to Google CLassroom and other Educational portals.

 I'm a teacher, why use Hodder Share?

Hodder Share links are permanent and safe. No more dead links or changes of final destination. Easily add them to the tools you use and see data about resource usage and link to similar resources.

I'm a small publisher, what's in it for me?

Hodder Share links are perfect for including in marketing and resource metadata publishing. Every Hodder Share links back to the original resource on the Publisher's site. Every time a Hodder Share is clicked generates valuable data about how the resource is used and shared.
