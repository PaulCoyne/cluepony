Services

Clue Pony is a new service that provides permanent, measurable links to educational resources. Only Clue Pony links let you see exactly how resources are used by teachers and students.

Resource linking

At Clue Pony, we believe that trusted, safe and permanent links to digital resources is essential to the discovery and usage of excellent educational content. That is why we help publishers create short URLs that teachers can easily incoporate into lessons. We’re committed to improving the discovery of educational content and understand that our job is to help our Publisher partners understand how their content is used in classrooms, so that they can create more of the content teachers really want.

Pre-print services for publishers

By using our Pre-Print Resources service, educational publishers can make in-production digital assets available to the education community before the printed book is actually published. It's a great way to get content in schools, driving sales and engagement around a title before it is launched . Contact us to learn more about our Pre-Print Pony IDs. 

Resource usage and impact

Clue Pony does its very best to supply publishers with new insights about how their content is being used. We provide Teacher's with free accounts and a range of tools to help them incorporate our publisher's content into Lessons as Clue Ponys. Our reports, generated from every clicked Clue Pony, signals that a resource was used in a classroom, not just viewed or downloaded. Contact us to learn how we can help teachers incorporate your content into their lessons.
